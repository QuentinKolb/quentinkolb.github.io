Target Audience

The target audience for this website will be for anyone who wants to learn more about the weather report and what they can do to be prepared for any unpredictable weather.

Persona 1

Jim is a 36 year-old father of 4 kids and is a hardworking business man. He is required to travel for work more often than he would like to. He does not like spending time away from his family, but knows that it is necessary in order to provide for them. One of the things that he likes least and his wife likes least is when he gets stuck in a far away state because of poor timing of his travel. If he had just flown home one day before, he would have avoided the storm and been able to be with his family. He needs a good weather source in order to be the family man he wants to be.

Persona 2

Mary is a 64 year-old active woman. Her and her husband enjoy going out on hikes around the town where they can reminice about their life together. They are increasing in age and decreasing in ability to get around on the trails if the weather does not permit it. Each of them have a degree in orinthology so they know when the right birds will be out in order for them to watch them. They need an accurate weather source to let them know when they should go on their hikes so they will be able to plan ahead and catch that Stresemann's Bristlefront they have always wanted to see.

Scenarios

  • What day should I fly so that I have the least possibility of getting stuck in snow?
  • When should we go hike R mountain today?
  • Will it be too windy to go hit a bucket of balls at the golf course?
  • When should I leave Rexburg and go home so that I do not get caught in a blizzard?
  • Should I ride my motorcycle to school today, or is it going to be to cold?
