Color Scheme

Dominant Color: Alabama Red

This color will be used in my title, headers, and footers. It also will be used to highlight any important information in the website. This maroon red allows people to be drawn to the headers and quickly find what they're looking for.

Complimentary Colors: Yellow Orange and Baby Blue

My navigation bar will be baby blue to offset the yellow orange. If anything else needs to be highlighted or set apart, I will use the yellow orange.
